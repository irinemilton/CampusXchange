How It Works

Circular Economy in 3 Steps

Our credit-based system eliminates the need for cash transactions while promoting sustainable resource sharing.

01

List Your Items

Upload unused textbooks, electronics, or lab equipment with a credit value. Your items become available to the entire campus community.

02

Request & Exchange

Browse the marketplace, find what you need, and send an exchange request. The item owner can accept or decline your offer.

03

Earn & Spend Credits

Givers earn credits, receivers spend them. The CreditLedger records every transaction with double-entry integrity.

Under The Hood

Powered by Advanced DBMS

This platform demonstrates sophisticated database concepts including ER modeling, normalization, ACID transactions, and multi-table referential integrity.

5 Entities

Student, Category, Item, ExchangeTransaction, CreditLedger — fully normalized to 3NF.

ACID Transactions

Atomic credit transfers with double-entry bookkeeping ensure financial data integrity.

State Machine

Items follow Available → Requested → Exchanged lifecycle with validation constraints.

Referential Integrity

Foreign key constraints maintain relational consistency across all five entities.
